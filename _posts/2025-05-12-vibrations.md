## Equations

The motion of a vibrating object is modelled by the equation

$$ mu'' + \gamma u' + ku = F(t) $$

Natural frequency: $\omega_0 = \sqrt{k/m}$

Sufficiently underdamped: $\gamma^2 < 2mk$

Static displacement: $F_0/k$
